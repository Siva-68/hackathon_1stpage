🔧 MobilityX Hackathon

🚀 Overview

The MobilityX Hackathon is a high-energy 24-hour event where students and techies tackle real-world mobility problems through creativity, coding, and collaboration.

🔍 Tracks / Themes

  • Smart Electric Vehicles (EVs)
  • AI in Transport and Mobility
  • Automotive IoT and Sensors
  • Sustainable and Green Technologies
  • Intelligent Traffic Systems

👥 Who Can Participate

Engineering Students, Developers, Researchers

Max Team Size: 6 members

🏰 What's at Stake

💰 Prize Pool: ₹40,000+

🎓 Certificates for all

🤝 Networking with mentors and experts

💡 How It Works – Step-by-Step

  • Registration & Team Formation: Register solo or in teams of up to 6. Choose your category (IEEE/Non-IEEE).
  • Problem Statement Release: Choose a real-world challenge provided on event day.
  • Development Begins: Use any tools or platforms to build your prototype.
  • Mentorship & Feedback: Experts help you refine ideas through mentoring sessions.
  • Overnight Support: Dedicated space, rest zones, and 24x7 tech assistance.
  • Submission & Pitching: Submit your solution, pitch it to judges, and showcase your prototype.
  • Results & Recognition: Win exciting prizes and get featured during the Conclave.
